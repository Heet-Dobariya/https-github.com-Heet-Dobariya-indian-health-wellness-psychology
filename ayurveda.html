Ayurveda

What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda is an ancient Indian system of medicine that emphasizes balance between body, mind, and spirit through diet, lifestyle, and natural remedies.

Principles

  • Vata - Movement & Air
  • Pitta - Fire & Digestion
  • Kapha - Structure & Stability
